ABOUT SPADEWORKS

Spadeworks is a sourcing, manufacturing, trading and export company in the field of clothing, PPE, textiles, leather and home furnishings. Spadeworks is a 14 year old company and its core strengths are supply-chain management, strong focus on execution and high level of quality control. Ethical sourcing coupled with strict adherence to International compliance standard is an essential component of Spadeworks philosophy.
